About the developer:

Kristen is from Boston and moved to the California Bay area in 2017. She now lives in a community of like minded individuals focused on inclusion, accessibiliy and human rights. She spends her free time focused on fitness, reading, coding and studying psychology. Pre pandemic she spent most of her free time dancing and orchestrated all womxn/female identifying contact jams in the Oakland area.

After 15 years as a stylist, team lead and national educator, she made the choice to enroll at Hackbright Academy in Feb. Years of working alongside software engineers inspired her and presented an opportunity to make a wider change in her community and challenge herself in something new.

Retame was created as a way to support her fellow stylists and barbers. The all too familiar question, "Do you remember what you did last time?" Though the memory of many stylists and barbers are incredible, a little support would provide more consistency for the client and the service provider while also being able to track your success.
